HOUSE OF CARDS

House of Cards is an American political drama television series created by Beau Willimon for the Netflix streaming service. It stars Kevin Spacey as Francis Underwood, an ambitious politician who aspires to high public office in Washington, D.C.. House of Cards is an adaptation of the eponymous novel written by Michael Dobbs and the British miniseries created by Andrew Davies. The thirteen-episode first season was made available in full on February 1, 2013. The second was released on February 14, 2014 with the same number of episodes. In Brazil, the series is also broadcast on the Paramount Channel and in Portugal the series is shown exclusively on Canal TVSéries. It was the first series broadcast in 4K resolution by Netflix, being succeeded by the remasters of Breaking Bad and Orange Is the New Black.
